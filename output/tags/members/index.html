Sadegh Sulaimany is Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Engineering in University of Kurdistan, Iran. He received his PhD in Bioinformatics from Tehran University in 2017. His Master and Bachelor degrees are in Computer Science and Engineering from Amirkabir and Isfahan University of Technology. His research interest lies in the biological and social network analysis, especially link prediction algorithms for different application areas.  He is always open to collaborations, and has many international colleagues with shared research projects.
